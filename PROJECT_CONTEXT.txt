# CleanShare - Project Context

## What is CleanShare?

CleanShare is a peer-to-peer marketplace where people can safely rent cleaning equipment from other people.

The platform connects equipment owners with people who need professional cleaning equipment for a short period of time.

---

## Mission

Help people generate extra income from cleaning equipment that spends most of its time unused.

At the same time, allow other people to access professional equipment without having to buy it.

---

## Target Users

Primary users:

- Owners of professional cleaning equipment worth between €200 and €2,500.
- Individuals.
- Small businesses.
- Cleaning professionals.

Examples:

- Pressure washers
- Carpet cleaners
- Upholstery cleaners
- Floor scrubbers
- Steam cleaners
- Wet & dry vacuums
- Window cleaning systems
- Industrial vacuum cleaners

---

## Main Value Proposition

The biggest challenge is NOT renting equipment.

The biggest challenge is making equipment owners feel completely safe.

CleanShare sells confidence.

Everything on the website should reinforce:

- Security
- Trust
- Simplicity
- Professionalism

---

## Pillars

### Security

Owners must feel their equipment is protected.

Examples:

- Security deposit
- Identity verification
- Digital contracts
- Insurance
- Secure payments

---

### Trust

Every interaction must increase trust.

Avoid anything that looks cheap or amateur.

The design should feel premium.

---

### Simplicity

The platform must look extremely easy to use.

Everything should require as few clicks as possible.

---

## Landing Page Goal

The purpose of this landing page is NOT to rent equipment.

The objective is to convince equipment owners to register and discover how much they can earn.

Every section should support this objective.

---

## Tone of Voice

Only modify the requested section.

Never redesign existing sections.

Always preserve the existing visual identity.

The website must communicate the same level of trust, quality and professionalism as successful technology companies.

Every design and development decision should support that vision.
